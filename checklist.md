## Action Descriptions (Standardized)
- [x] Shoot description: As an Action, Make a ranged attack with the [weapon name]. On hit: [weapon damage] + your [weapon modifier(s)].
- [x] Strike description: As an Action, Make a melee attack with the [weapon name]. On hit: [weapon damage] + your [weapon modifier(s)].
- [x] Hilt Strike description: As a Bonus Action, Make a melee attack with the hilt of [weapon name], possibly dazing them. On hit: The target takes 1d4 kinetic damage and must make a Constitution saving throw: DC:8 + proficiency bonus + your [weapon modifier(s)]. On a failed save, the target becomes dazed (dazed: has disadvantage on Wisdom saving throws, can't take Reactions and loses the Dexterity bonus to their AC). On a successful save, the target doesn't get dazed.
- [x] Slash description: As an Action, Make a sweeping melee attack with the [weapon name] against all creatures standing in a 15 foot horizontal line originating from you. Each target makes a Dexterity saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your [weapon modifier(s)]). A creature takes [weapon damage] without modifiers on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
- [x] Back Strike description: As a Bonus Action, Make a melee attack with the back end of the [weapon name]. On hit: [weapon damage] without modifiers.
- [x] Off-Hand Strike description: As a Bonus Action, Make a melee attack with the [weapon name] while wielding it in your off-hand. On hit: [weapon damage] without modifiers.
- [x] Quick Shot's description: As a Bonus Action, Make a quick hipfire shot with the [weapon name] while wielding it in your off-hand. On hit: [weapon damage] without modifiers.
- [x] Flame description: As an Action, Flame multiple targets in a [weapon range] cone in front of you. Each target must make a Dexterity saving throw, DC 8 + proficiency bonus (if applicable) + Dexterity modifier. On a failed save, the target takes [weapon damage] + [weapon modifier(s)] and is inflicted burning for 2 turns. On a successful save, the target takes half damage and is inflicted burning for 1 turn.
- [x] Dart description: As an Action, Shoot a Dart with the [weapon name] at a target. On hit: Damage and conditions depend on the dart.
- [x] Rocket description: As an Action, Shoot a Rocket with the [weapon name] at a target. On hit: Damage and conditions depend on the rocket.
